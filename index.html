Fresno Transit Free

This was a group class project I was involved at Fresno City College. We end up using the iphone version of this project to enter the 2013 59DaysOfCode and we actually tied for the People's Choice Award :D. In this work I was involve with coding the database and the transiting of screens. I mostly worked on Android version but I did work a little on the iphone version on the database. On the iphone I mainly did work involving php and sending out gps pings inorder to show proof of concept for the eventual live feed.

There were some couple of nifty things this app has that may not be obvious but the main points of this app that the data is stored locally so a person can at least get the bus schedule printed on the booklets without internet. Another nifty part is that you can save bus routes for quick access. The interface we worked to try to make it as easy as possible to flip between times and visual map of the bus route that is even easier than the bus schedule paper booklet.

We are currently working on a version that is more elaborate and involving live feed as well providing accessiblity.
